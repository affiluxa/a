Who We Are

Affiluxa is an independent skill development initiative focused on fiber-optic, FTTH, and telecom field training.

Our programs are designed to provide real, hands-on skills required in telecom field operations.

100% FREE Training • 100% FREE Certificates • No Hidden Charges

To empower youth with job-ready telecom skills while removing financial barriers to technical education.

What We Offer

  • ✔ FREE verifiable digital certificates
